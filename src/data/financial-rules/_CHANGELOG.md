# Financial Rules Changelog

## 2026-05-24 — Initial population

**India:**
- `india-tax-new-regime.json` — New regime slabs per Union Budget 2026-27 (no changes from FY 2025-26). Standard deduction Rs 75,000. Rebate u/s 87A Rs 60,000 for income up to Rs 12L.
- `india-tax-old-regime.json` — Old regime slabs per Union Budget 2026-27 (no changes from prior year). Standard deduction Rs 50,000. Rebate u/s 87A Rs 12,500 for income up to Rs 5L.
- `india-section-80c.json` — Rs 1,50,000 limit (unchanged since FY 2014-15). No change in Budget 2026-27.
- `india-section-80d.json` — Rs 25,000 (self/family below 60) / Rs 50,000 (senior). No change in Budget 2026-27.
- `india-capital-gains.json` — LTCG 12.5% / STCG 20% on equity per Finance Act 2024 (effective 23-Jul-2024). No change in Budget 2026-27.
- `india-gst-rates.json` — GST 2.0 structure effective 22-Sep-2025 per 56th GST Council meeting. Slabs: 0%, 5%, 18%, 40% (main). 3% for gold. 0.25% for rough diamonds. 28% for tobacco.
- `india-epf-rates.json` — 8.25% for FY 2026-27 per EPFO Central Board of Trustees. Unchanged from FY 2024-25 and FY 2025-26.
- `india-ppf-rates.json` — 7.1% for Q1 FY 2026-27 (Apr-Jun 2026) per DEA notification 30-Mar-2026. Unchanged since April 2020.

**United States:**
- `us-tax-brackets.json` — 2026 inflation-adjusted brackets per IRS Revenue Procedure 2025-32. Standard deductions updated; senior $6,000 additional deduction per One Big Beautiful Bill Act.
- `us-401k-limits.json` — 2026 limits per IRS IR-2025-111. 401k employee limit $24,500 (up from $23,500). IRA $7,500 (up from $7,000). SECURE 2.0 catch-up for age 60-63: $11,250.
